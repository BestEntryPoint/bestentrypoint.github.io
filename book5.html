What is all this Crypto about?
 Things are changing fast.


Probably, we are turning out to the world where wealth will be reduced to pure information: where things — like state money, buildings, lands, gold and so on — will not be valued the most.

It will be so since more and more people are starting to understand that the most valuable thing in the universe is only the ability to be limited — limited in the amount.

With keeping it in mind a virtual coin Bitcoin was created. The word virtual here means that this coin exists only in minds of the computers and we cannot even feel this coin by our hands, for example.

Probably, Bitcoin is the most useful thing in the whole world, since — is really limited in the amount.

So probably, we are turning out to the world where a thing that cannot be even felt will cost THE MOST.

Where you can move your money freely, around the world, without needing any permission. Even, without needing your Identity Document.

Where no State can forcibly take away your money, i.e. your wealth.

In our opinion, we will enevatably be there. It is only a matter of time. Nearest time.

All it will happen thanks to crypto coins — cryptocurrencies.
