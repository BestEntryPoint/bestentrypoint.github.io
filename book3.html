What is Robotics indeed?
 Robotics is the creation of magic — it is so, not being a hyperbole.

What really happens is that we take dead materials — metals and some other materials — next, combine them, and we get some ... thing, some dead organism.

Next, we leanch blood into this organism — in the technological world, blood is electrical current — so we leanch electrical current into this organism and it becomes alive.

We get an alive organism which, really, can understand us. And we can command it.

So programming, electronics, or robotics is when we vitalize dead things and then command them.

Robots are slaves of the technological era. All works on Earth, as far as possible, should be done by robots — by modern slaves.

People, in turn, should just enjoy their lives, doing creative things and pleasant activities. Of course, self-development as well.

So you, as a kid, may really command vitalized beings — alive beings of a special sort.

Together, you with robots, can do a lot.

The imagination is the limit.
