Best Entry Point

We believe that the most hard thing in starting anything is to do the first step. We want to make this step really easy. Totally!

Hopefully, it is the first easily understandable book on Robotics for girls and boys age 8+. Also, the best entry point for anyone more adult who wants to start commanding Robots.

By the way, we practice redundancy throughout this book, using very simple words and explaining the same things several times.


What is Robotics indeed?
 Robotics is the process of making magic — it is so, not being a hyperbole.

What really happens is that we take alive organisms created by other people from dead materials — metals and some other materials — and we command them by writing some texts.

So we have alive organisms created from dead materials which, really, can understand us. And we can command them by writing some texts.

Is not it magic? It is, absolutelly.

So Robotics is when we vitalize dead things and then command them.

Robots are slaves of the technological era. All works on Earth, as far as possible, should be done by Robots — by modern slaves.

People, in turn, should just enjoy their lives, doing creative things and pleasant activities. Of course, self-development as well.

So you, as a young person, may really command vitalized beings — alive beings of a special sort.

Together, you with Robots, can do a lot.

The imagination is the limit.

Two ways of each aspect
 Aspect One.

There are two ways to learn how to command Robots:
1. to spend a lot of money and remain stupid
2. to spend less money and become smarter step be step

The first way is represented by, for example, Lego clubs and the like communities.

The second way is represented by Arduino and Raspberry Pi communities.

The second way just takes more will from you — in other words, takes more patience from you.

We here provide the second way for you.

Aspect Two.

There are two ways to buy necessary things (components):
1. to spend a lot of money and buy originals — expensive way
2. to spend less money and buy replicas — thrifty way

The second way just takes more will from you — in other words, takes more patience from you.

We here provide both the ways for you — first and second.

Our first goal

As our 1-st goal let's create a Robot which constantly winks at us with its eye — one time in 5 seconds.

Or, one time in 3 seconds — only what you want.

Let's get on it.

Don't be afraid
 Do not be afraid of all electronic things — all components or modules. You do not need to understand how they work. Treat them as so-called black boxes. You need to just know what you may get from them. And also know what you have to do in order to get from them what you want.

If you extremely crave to know how electronic things work, then congratulations and just go to Electronics.
